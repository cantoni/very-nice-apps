Philosophy

START SMALL AND STAY LEAN. Keep teams and features minimal. Focus on doing more with less and cut the fat.

REMIX THE BEST. Use proven ideas from successful businesses. Adapt what works and make it your own.

CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COMES FIRST. No customers, no business. Find users, then figure out the rest.

MAKE WEIRD STUFF. Build bold, surprising products that people talk about. Use AI and tech to stand out.

KILL YOUR DARLINGS. Cut what doesn’t work. Test big ideas early, move fast, and don’t waste time.
